Meet the Founder

Saviour Lawrence Inyang is the visionary behind SLI RESOURCES. With a solid academic foundation in Chemistry from Akwa Ibom State University, he has dedicated his life to transforming ideas into impactful technology for local communities. His career reflects passion, purpose, and progressive thinking.

With years of experience in hardware design, IoT, and automation systems, Saviour founded SLI RESOURCES to bridge gaps in energy, water, and automation using affordable smart tools. His vision of African-made global innovations drives the company’s core operations.

He is also a public speaker, mentor, and researcher, collaborating with institutions and start-ups to accelerate sustainable development across Africa. When he’s not building prototypes, he’s inspiring young talents to believe in the power of invention.
