Hydrolynk - Smart Water Pump Controller

Hydrolynk is the revolutionary solution for automated water management. Developed to suit the Nigerian environment, Hydrolynk automatically controls your water pump to prevent overflow or dry-running. This ensures safety, efficiency, and peace of mind.

With built-in water level sensors and a rugged housing, Hydrolynk is ideal for homes, hostels, offices, and facilities that use boreholes or tanks. No more forgetting to turn off the pump or climbing tanks to check water levels. It does the work for you—automatically.
