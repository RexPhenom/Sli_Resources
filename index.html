Hydrolynk - Smart Pump Control

Hydrolynk is an automatic pump switch that helps households, schools, and companies control their borehole or tank pumps automatically. It starts or stops the pump based on real-time tank water levels and protects the pump from dry runs. Durable, affordable, and easy to install.
