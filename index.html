Hydrolynk

Hydrolynk is our flagship product: an intelligent water pump controller that takes the guesswork out of water management. Designed with microcontroller-based logic, Hydrolynk automatically turns pumps on and off based on water levels in the tank, saving energy and preventing overflow or dry running.

Key Features:
• Automatic ON/OFF switching
• Dry-run protection
• Tank-level sensors
• LED status display
• Compatible with overhead and underground tanks
• Easy to install and maintain

With Hydrolynk, managing your water supply is now smarter, safer, and more efficient.
