Hydrolynk - Smart Water Pump Controller

Hydrolynk is our flagship product and Nigeria's first locally-developed automatic water pumping system with intelligent control. Designed for households, hotels, hostels, and schools, it ensures your tank never runs dry and your pump never runs dry either.

With sensor-based control and auto shutoff, Hydrolynk prevents common water wastage, reduces energy bills, and increases pump life span. It comes with a mobile notification module (coming soon), easy-to-install design, and operates silently with rugged weatherproof casing.
