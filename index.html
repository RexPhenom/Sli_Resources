Hydrolynk - Smart Water Controller

Hydrolynk is our flagship smart water pump control system. It brings automation to your water system, ensuring seamless tank monitoring, automatic refill control, and pump safety protection. Designed for both domestic and industrial use, Hydrolynk is ideal for houses, farms, schools, and hotels.

Features include:

  • Automatic start/stop based on water tank level
  • Dry-run pump protection
  • Energy-efficient operation
  • Durable and weatherproof casing
  • LED indicators and troubleshooting feedback

With Hydrolynk, managing water becomes hands-free and worry-free. It’s affordable, easy to install, and built with the user in mind.
