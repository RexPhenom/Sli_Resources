Hydrolynk Smart Water Pump Control

Hydrolynk is our flagship smart water pump control system engineered to solve common water management issues in homes, farms, and industrial setups. The device uses advanced sensors to detect water levels and automates the switching of water pumps, eliminating dry running, overflows, and human error.

Key features include wireless remote monitoring, solar support, low power consumption, and compatibility with existing water systems. Hydrolynk is user-friendly, highly durable, and perfect for regions with irregular power or water supply.
