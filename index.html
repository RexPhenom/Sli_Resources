Hydrolynk - Smart Water Controller

Hydrolynk is a smart water pump automation system that turns your ordinary water system into an intelligent one. With automatic ON/OFF control based on tank levels, energy saving, and protection against pump damage, it’s perfect for homes, farms, hotels, and hospitals.

It works silently, efficiently, and gives you peace of mind. Never worry about your tank overflowing or running dry again.
